Video Podcast Series

Discover Iran takes viewers on a visual journey through the vastly different regions, cultures and traditions of Iran. In travel diary style, the podcast series filmed over 6 months time span, reveals famous as well as unique and often unseen corners of Iran’s rich historical, cosmopolitan and geographical landscape. Stay tuned for regular episodes!
 

Regions Covered

  • Southeastern Iran
  • Persian Gulf
  • Central Iran
  • Northeastern Iran
  • Caspian Sea
  • Western Iran
  • Capital city, Tehran
